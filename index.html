ABOUT US

Little Voices was initiated in 2010 when a group of friends came together with the intention of giving back to the society. They were inspired by the thought and philosophy of Peter Senge, the founder of the Society for Organizational Learning who has propagated that “sustainability, social equality and the environment are now business problems…” and corporate leaders can’t expect governments to solve them alone.
It is an Orphange-Based Non-profit Organisation which has suceeded in serving more than 13,000 people in past seven years.
Little Voices is a platform where connection and contribution between and by the people is made easier and tustable.
